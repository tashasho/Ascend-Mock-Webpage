FAQs

Everything you need to know about joining Z5 Ascend.

What is the difference between the two tracks?

The Startup Track is for pre-formed teams (2+ co-founders) with an MVP/Prototype who are ready to scale. You receive $40k investment.

The HPI Track is for individuals (engineers, domain experts) who haven't found a co-founder yet. You receive a $5k grant and structured matching support. If you form a team, you become eligible for the investment.

Is the program remote or in-person?

It is a hybrid model. Weeks 1-3 are virtual (remote), allowing you to participate from anywhere in India. Week 4 is a fully immersive in-person week in the San Francisco Bay Area (Silicon Valley).

Do you cover travel costs for the immersion week?

Yes. Z5 Capital covers travel (flights) and accommodation costs for the Immersion Week in Silicon Valley for accepted founders.

What is the selection process timeline?

The discovery and application phase lasts 4-6 weeks. Once applications close, we have a 2-week selection period involving committee reviews and virtual interviews before announcing the final cohort.
